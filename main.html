Favorite Holiday Destination

Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is famous for its beautiful architecture, romantic ambiance, and rich cultural heritage. Visitors flock here to explore iconic landmarks, enjoy exquisite cuisine, and immerse themselves in art and history.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris with stunning views from the top.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – A charming neighborhood with artistic vibes and scenic streets.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue for shopping and luxurious cafes.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky and buttery French pastry, perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette – Traditional French bread with a crispy crust.
  • Escargot – A delicacy of garlic-flavored snails.
  • Crème Brûlée – Rich custard dessert with a caramelized top.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate almond-based cookies with sweet fillings.
